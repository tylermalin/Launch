9. Glossary

Personal Data

Information that identifies, relates to, describes, or can reasonably be linked to an individual.

Controller

The entity that determines the purposes and means of processing Personal Data.

Processor

A service provider or other party that processes Personal Data on behalf of a controller.

Legitimate Interests

Our legitimate business interests in operating, improving, securing, and growing our products and services, provided those interests are not overridden by your rights and interests.

Performance of Contract

Processing necessary to enter into or perform a contract with you.

Legal Obligation

Processing necessary to comply with applicable law, regulation, or lawful request.

Blockchain Data

Publicly visible and often immutable data recorded on distributed ledger networks.
