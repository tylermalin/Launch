Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What exactly does the $2,000 entry cost include?
Hardware ($380) covers a Raspberry Pi Zero 2W, atmospheric sensors, weatherproof enclosure, solar UPS, and secure enclave. License ($1,620) covers exclusive geographic rights to your hex, Genesis 200 MLMA allocation, platform infrastructure, and 12 months of support. You pay once at reserve (May 2026). Hardware ships in September 2026.
Q: When do I receive my 125K MLMA tokens?
Vesting happens in two phases: 25% (31,250 MLMA) vests when your hardware boots and you register your node (late September - early October 2026). The remaining 75% (93,750 MLMA) vests linearly over 12 months at ~7,813 MLMA/month. After 12 months, all tokens are unlocked and you can hold, stake, or sell them.
Q: How quickly will I recover my $2,000 entry cost?

All scenarios assume validation rewards accrue from early-mid October onward at typical $0.10-0.20/MLMA prices. See "Year 1 Earnings" section above for detailed scenarios.
Q: When do I start earning rewards?
Reserve by May 31, 2026. Hardware ships in September. You deploy and boot in late September/early October. First MLMA rewards accrue in early-mid October 2026 (once SaveCard validations begin flowing to the network). Full detail in the timeline section above.
Q: Why only 200 nodes?
Scarcity ensures geographic multiplier premium and prevents oversupply. Helium deployed 500K+ nodes and collapsed operator returns. We are demand-first: 200 nodes in key zones with pre-screened carbon projects, Smart City Demand, AI Data Center locations or active Climate Prediction Markets which our Nodes can offer Settlement. Genesis 200 ends when sold or June 2026. Phase 2 (2027+) expands at lower initial multipliers.
Q: How do Geographic Multipliers work?
Your hex location (H3 Resolution 5, ~252.9 km²) determines your reward multiplier. Urban zones earn 0.5×, suburban 1.0×, rural 1.5×, frontier 2.0×, strategic up to 3.0×. High-demand zones (Idaho ERW, LA forestry, NYC emissions, London Article 6.4) are allocated first and earn premium multipliers due to validation volume.
Q: What is the Indigenous Stewardship program?
If your hex overlaps Indigenous territory (UN self-identification + community acceptance), stewards receive 10-25% of your node rewards (default 15%). Settlement is monthly in MLMA or fiat (USD, EUR, BRL, etc.) at your choice. Mālama covers all conversion costs. Disputes resolved via prediction market + DAO governance.
